Supun-Maximu Services

Weight Management

When you manage your weight, it brings you good health now and forever. Conversely, those who have excess weight (obesity) may have a high chance of being affected by many severe diseases and health conditions. Supun-Maximus program for weight management, helps you to possess an ideal weight and attractive appearance.

Strength & Conditioning

Strength and Conditioning is a combined development of static and dynamic activities to improve physical performance. Based on the athlete’s sports and area of specialisation, Supun-Maximus develops a systematic tailor-made program to bring out desired performance levels.

Injury Prevention & Rehabilitation

Professional athletes, sports activities and even anyone may suffer from an injury. Supun-Maximus injury prevention and rehabilitation programs are designed to ensure the athlete will have a faster recovery and resume their training with confidence. It brings benefits to the athlete by improving posture, strengthening the core and flexibility to minimize the chances of injury.

Cross-Fit Training

It is a kind of high-intensity interval training that aims at strength and conditioning of your body using movements you perform in day-to-day life. Supun-Maximus Cross-Fit Training programs are designed with many types of weightlifting, push-ups and squats that are performed for a predetermined time for building your muscles.
